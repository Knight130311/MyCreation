Steps to Make Coffee

  1. Boil Water
  2. Grind Coffee Beans
  3. Place Ground Coffee in Filter
  4. Pour Hot Water over Coffee
  5. Stir and Enjoy!

Coffee Brewing Methods

  • French Press
  • Drip Coffee Maker
  • AeroPress
  • Espresso Machine
  • Pour Over

Coffee Terms

Espresso
A concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots.
Latte
A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.
Cappuccino
Similar to a latte, but with more foam and often topped with cocoa.
Americano
Espresso diluted with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee.
